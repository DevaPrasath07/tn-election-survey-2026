Methodology Statement

Transparency Note: This is a private, non-political interest survey conducted by independent student.

1. Sampling Method

We use a Digital Volunteer Sampling method. Participants voluntarily visit the website to record their opinion.

2. Verification Process (Updated)

To ensure the integrity of the survey and prevent multiple votes from a single person:

  • Email Verification: Every vote is verified using a One-Time Password (OTP) sent to a valid email address.
  • One Vote Per Email: Our system strictly enforces a "One Person, One Vote" policy based on unique email IDs.

3. Privacy & Anonymity

While we collect email addresses for verification, individual voting choices are aggregated. We do not publish who voted for whom.

4. Data Limitation

This survey represents the views of the digitally active population of Tamil Nadu and may not fully reflect the views of the offline rural population.
