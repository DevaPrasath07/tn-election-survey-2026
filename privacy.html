Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum amount of data necessary to verify your vote:

  • Email Address: Used strictly to send a verification OTP and prevent duplicate voting.
  • Vote Preference: Your selected political preference.
  • Timestamp: The time your vote was cast.

2. How We Use Your Data

We DO NOT sell your data.

Your email address is used solely for authentication purposes. It is cross-referenced only to ensure that the same email ID does not vote twice. It is not shared with any political party, candidate, or marketing agency.

3. Data Retention

All personal identifiers (email addresses) will be permanently deleted from our servers 30 days after the election results are announced.

4. Your Rights

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, you have the right to request the deletion of your data at any time by contacting the administrator.
